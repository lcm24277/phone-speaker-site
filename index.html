常见问题FAQ

解答您可能遇到的问题Answers to questions you may have

什么是"手机变音箱"？What is "Phone Speaker"?

"手机变音箱"是一款可以将您的安卓手机变成电脑外放音箱的工具软件。通过无线连接，让您的手机播放电脑的声音，无需额外的音频线或音箱设备。"Phone Speaker" is a tool software that can turn your Android phone into an external speaker for your computer. Through wireless connection, your phone can play computer sound without additional audio cables or speaker devices.

使用"手机变音箱"需要什么条件？What are the requirements for using "Phone Speaker"?

您需要一台运行 Windows 10/11 的电脑和一部安卓手机，两者需要连接在同一无线网络下。电脑需要安装我们的电脑版软件，手机需要安装安卓版应用。You need a computer running Windows 10/11 and an Android phone, both connected to the same wireless network. The computer needs to install our PC software, and the phone needs to install the Android app.

安卓端的作用是什么？What is the function of the Android app?

安卓端应用负责接收电脑发送的音频数据并播放出来。它相当于一个无线接收器和播放器，将电脑的声音通过手机的扬声器播放出来。The Android app is responsible for receiving audio data sent from the computer and playing it. It acts as a wireless receiver and player, playing computer sound through the phone's speaker.

网络要求是什么？必须使用同一个 WiFi 吗？What are the network requirements? Must use the same WiFi?

为了获得最佳体验，我们建议电脑和手机连接在同一个 WiFi 网络下。虽然也可以通过移动热点等方式连接，但同一 WiFi 网络下的连接稳定性和音质会更好。For the best experience, we recommend that the computer and phone are connected to the same WiFi network. Although it can also be connected through mobile hotspots, the connection stability and sound quality will be better under the same WiFi network.

如果连接失败怎么办？What if the connection fails?

如果连接失败，请尝试以下步骤：1. 确保电脑和手机在同一网络下；2. 检查防火墙是否阻止了连接；3. 重启电脑端和手机端应用；4. 重启路由器。如果问题仍然存在，请联系我们的客服。If the connection fails, please try the following steps: 1. Ensure that the computer and phone are on the same network; 2. Check if the firewall is blocking the connection; 3. Restart the computer and phone applications; 4. Restart the router. If the problem persists, please contact our customer service.

使用过程中会有延迟吗？Will there be delay during use?

由于无线传输的特性，可能会有轻微的延迟（通常在100ms以内）。对于一般的音乐播放、视频观看等场景，这种延迟通常不会被察觉。但对于游戏等对声音同步要求极高的场景，可能会有所感知。Due to the characteristics of wireless transmission, there may be a slight delay (usually within 100ms). For general music playback, video watching and other scenarios, this delay is usually not noticeable. But for games and other scenarios that require extremely high sound synchronization, it may be perceived.

可以替代传统音箱吗？Can it replace traditional speakers?

"手机变音箱"主要是作为临时解决方案或在没有音箱的情况下使用。虽然现代手机的扬声器质量已经很好，但在音质、音量和低音表现等方面，专业音箱仍然有优势。对于日常使用和临时需求，手机完全可以胜任。"Phone Speaker" is mainly used as a temporary solution or when there are no speakers available. Although the speaker quality of modern phones is already very good, professional speakers still have advantages in terms of sound quality, volume and bass performance. For daily use and temporary needs, the phone is completely capable.

iOS 系统对音频处理有更严格的限制，开发难度较大。我们正在积极研发 iOS 版本，预计将在未来3-6个月内推出。请持续关注我们的官网获取最新消息。iOS system has stricter restrictions on audio processing, making development more difficult. We are actively developing the iOS version, which is expected to be released within the next 3-6 months. Please continue to follow our official website for the latest news.

使用"手机变音箱"安全吗？会有隐私问题吗？Is it safe to use "Phone Speaker"? Are there privacy issues?

我们非常重视用户隐私和数据安全。"手机变音箱"仅传输音频数据，不会收集或上传您的个人信息。所有数据传输都在本地网络内进行，不会经过我们的服务器。我们的软件也不会获取您手机的敏感权限。We attach great importance to user privacy and data security. "Phone Speaker" only transmits audio data and does not collect or upload your personal information. All data transmission is carried out within the local network and does not go through our servers. Our software also does not obtain sensitive permissions from your phone.

是否支持多台手机同时连接一台电脑？Does it support multiple phones connecting to one computer at the same time?

目前版本暂不支持多台手机同时连接一台电脑。我们正在考虑在未来版本中添加这一功能，让您可以将声音输出到多个手机上，实现类似多房间音箱的效果。The current version does not support multiple phones connecting to one computer at the same time. We are considering adding this feature in future versions, allowing you to output sound to multiple phones, achieving an effect similar to multi-room speakers.
